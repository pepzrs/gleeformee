Papa's Pizzeria

Papa's Pizzeria is a typical cooking and restaurant management game where you take on the role of Roy and try to serve the best pizzas to the customers, make them happy, and earn money during the time Papa Louie leaves town.

What is Papa's Pizzeria game

Papa's Pizzeria is a part of the Papa Louie restaurant management game series. Papa Louie is the chef king. He opens many restaurants around the world. Now he has to leave the town for his business. And you have to help him run the pizza restaurant until he is back. Try to serve the customers and create delicious pizza to make them happy. You will need to take care of the whole process of running a restaurant, from taking orders and baking pizzas, to upgrading the restaurant. 

Like in other cooking games of the series, your mission is to serve the best pizza for the customers. When they are satisfied, you can earn more money. So work hard and smart to grow the restaurant and make Papa Louie proud. Your adventure starts now. Are you ready?

How to play Papa's Pizzeria game

Make yummy pizza

In this game, your main goal is to take orders and make delicious pizzas following the requirements. Then, you can earn money to expand the business and upgrade your restaurant. To do that, you have to perform different tasks. The first one is to take orders, write them down, and remember them so that you can complete the orders exactly. Then, you have to move to different stations to prepare the pizza. For example, you have to put it in the oven, add toppings, and slice it. Try to do your best to unlock new regular customers. 

Know how to use different stations

There are four different stations that you need to know about. Each station serves different purposes in the game.

The Order Station - This is the first station that you need to know. When the customers arrive at the restaurant, they will give orders. This is where you receive orders, write them down, and remember them. 

The Topping Station - After taking orders, you need to move to this station and start the process of making pizza. You can start by adding different kinds of toppings that the customers required. You can find meatballs, peppers, olives, onions, and many other ingredients to add to the pizza. Each customer wants different kinds of toppings, and you have to pay close attention to that. 

The Baking Station - Once you've added toppings, it's time to put them in the oven. Remember that each customer wants the pizza to be baked in the oven for a certain amount of time. You need to bake the pizza during that certain period. Otherwise, they will become too soft or too crisp for them. Additionally, you can bake up to four pizzas at the same time. 

The Cutting Station - When the pizza is done baking, you have to cut it into certain pieces and give them to the customers. 

Manage your time and orders wisely

You may find the game easy at first because you don't need to rush to complete the orders and there are just a few customers coming to the restaurant. However, as you go further in the game, you will need to handle more customers with more complex requirements. This is the time that you can show off your time-management skills. You need to balance taking orders and making pizzas.

It is important to choose which orders to prepare first because some customers want the pizza to be baked for a longer amount of time than others. Try not to let them wait too long. Or else, they will leave the restaurant. So to win the game, you need to have great time management skills and pay attention to the whole process of making pizza. Try not to miss any details. Otherwise, you may fail to complete the order. 

Finally, with the money you've earned, you can purchase equipment, and furniture, and upgrade the restaurant to work more efficiently and attract more customers. Good luck and have fun playing!

Controls

You can use your left mouse button to play and interact in the game.
